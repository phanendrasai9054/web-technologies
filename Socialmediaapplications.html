Social Media Applications And Their Features

  • WhatsApp

    • Instant messaging application.
    • Send text messages, photos, videos, and voice messages.
    • Voice and video calling.
    • Group chats and broadcast lists.
    • End-to-end encryption.

  • YouTube

    • Video sharing platform.
    • Upload and watch videos.
    • Subscribe to channels.
    • Live streaming capabilities.
    • Create and share playlists.

  • LinkedIn

    • Professional networking site.
    • Connect with colleagues and industry professionals.
    • Share professional updates and articles.
    • Job search and recruiting platform.
    • Endorsements and recommendations.

  • Instagram

    • Photo and video sharing platform.
    • Stories feature for 24-hour content.
    • IGTV for long-format videos.
    • Direct messaging and video calls.
    • Explore page for discovering new content.

  • Twitter

    • Real-time microblogging platform.
    • Tweet text, photos, and videos.
    • Follow topics and hashtags.
    • Retweet and like posts.
    • Direct messaging and lists.
